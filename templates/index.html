Los Angeles County Traffic Collisions Analysis

EXPLORE BELOW

On Average 3,287 people die each DAY just from car collisions.

Men participate in more collisions than females in LA County

Traffic collisions are one of the leading causes of death in the United States.

For this reason traffic collisions are a great candidate for study. By analyzing different factors involved in traffic collisions, we may unveil invaluable insights about these occurances. We chose to analyze traffic collissions in Los Angeles County (LA), as it is a large commuter city. The graphs were made utilizing data from Los Angeles City for the year of 2018.

Overview of Accidents in 2018



Gender Analysis

The pie chart serates participants in collisions by gender.

The majorioty of accidents involved men.





Age Analysis

The bar graph seperates the collisions by age.

Drivers in thier twenties participated in more traffic collisions than drivers in other age groups.



Demographic Analysis

Herein is an illustration of the demographics of people who participated in car accidents.

The Hispanic/Latino population accounted for 39% of accidents. This may be due to the large Hispanic/Latino population in Los Angeles.



Week Analysis

The bar graph seperates the collisions by day of the week.

Fridays are a pretty exciting day of the week, however the highest frequency of incidents occured on fridays. Take caution when driving on friday afternoons.
